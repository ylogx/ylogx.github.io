Work

I am Shubham Chaudhary a software developer in the recommendations team of Scribd - the Netflix for books and audiobooks. Previously, I worked in the Machine Learning team of Zomato. I worked on projects involving NLP, deep learning based models for image classification & image quality assessment, scalable duplicate detection for text documents and images, restaurant rating algorithm and user credibility.
